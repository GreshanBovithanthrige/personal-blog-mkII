Multiple BSODs and random freezes.


Multiple BSODs and random freezes. Random freezes came with a failing hard drive but this BSOD came with an outdated AMD driver. One of the BSODs came with a "win30kfull.sys". and as you can see some of the words have misspellings.

.sys files are coming with Win Operating System, Most of the .sys files are related to device drivers. These drivers keep the communication between your PC hardware and the Windows System intact. "win30kfull.sys" is one of Kernel-mode device drivers, any .sys failure can cause serious damage, most commonly BSOD errors like the one we have here..

if you get one of these try updating your drivers and if your Windows system has any updates pending, try that. Then try a system file checker. If the issue persists try testing your RAMs and Disks. Reinstall any new program you installed. One of these steps should be the fix for this.
